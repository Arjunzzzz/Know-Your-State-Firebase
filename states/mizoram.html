Mizoram

Mizoram, located in the northeastern part of India, is known for its breathtaking natural landscapes and vibrant cultural traditions.
Often called the "Land of Blue Mountains," Mizoram boasts rolling hills, lush greenery, and serene surroundings that captivate visitors.
The state is celebrated for its rich tribal heritage, with festivals such as Chapchar Kut and Thalfavang Kut showcasing traditional music, dance, and cuisine.
Attractions like the picturesque town of Aizawl, the stunning Phawngpui Peak, and the tranquil Reiek Heritage Village offer unique and enriching experiences.
