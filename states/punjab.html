Punjab

Punjab, a state in northern India, is known for its vibrant culture and rich heritage.
The region is famous for its lush landscapes, historic gurdwaras, and hearty cuisine.
Key landmarks include the Golden Temple in Amritsar, Jallianwala Bagh, and the Wagah Border.
Punjab's lively festivals, such as Baisakhi and Lohri, highlight its rich traditions and communal spirit.
With its agricultural prosperity and cultural vibrancy, Punjab is a region of immense significance.
