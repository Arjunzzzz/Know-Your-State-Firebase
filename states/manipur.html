Manipur

Manipur, located in northeastern India, is known for its stunning natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage.
Often referred to as the "Jewell of the East," the state features picturesque lakes, rolling hills, and vibrant floral beauty.
Manipur is renowned for its traditional dance forms, such as Manipuri dance, and its lively festivals like Yaoshang and Ningol Chakouba.
The state's scenic spots, including Loktak Lake and the beautiful valley of Imphal, offer visitors unique and enriching experiences.
With its blend of cultural richness and natural beauty, Manipur is a captivating destination that offers a memorable experience.
