Bihar

Situated in the eastern part of India, Bihar is a land steeped in historical significance and spiritual heritage.
Known for its ancient monuments, including the revered sites of Bodh Gaya, where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment, and Nalanda, the ancient seat of learning, Bihar offers a glimpse into India's rich past.
The state is blessed with the serene banks of the Ganges River and is home to numerous pilgrimage sites, vibrant festivals, and a rich tradition of folk arts.
With its historical sites, lush green landscapes, and cultural richness, Bihar stands as a testament to India's enduring legacy and spiritual depth.
