Meghalaya

Meghalaya, located in northeastern India, is celebrated for its stunning natural beauty and unique landscapes.
Known as the "Abode of Clouds," the state features breathtaking scenery, including lush green hills, cascading waterfalls, and scenic valleys.
Meghalaya is renowned for its vibrant festivals, such as the Wangala and Shad Suk Mynsiem, which highlight the rich cultural traditions of the region.
The state also offers amazing experiences with its unique living root bridges, serene lakes like Umiam, and the pristine beauty of Cherrapunji.
Meghalaya's welcoming people and diverse natural attractions make it a remarkable destination for nature lovers and cultural enthusiasts alike.
